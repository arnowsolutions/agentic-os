## New Appointment Documentation

### Prepared by the Faculty Candidate
1. Completed and signed Faculty Appointment Application
2. Current curriculum vitae and bibliography
3. Current NYS Medical License Registration (if applicable)
4. American Board Certification Certificate(s) (if applicable)
5. Doctoral Degree(s) copy, or highest degree
6. Signed Authorization to Release Information Form
7. Conflict of Interest (COI) disclosure form (submitted via email from COI office after processing)
8. Einstein COI CITI course (for payroll faculty)

### Prepared by the Academic Department
1. Completed and signed Faculty Appointment Department Recommendation Form
2. Signed letter of commitment from departmental chair (Einstein salaried only)

**Important**: No individual should use their proposed title until approved in writing.

### Joint Appointments
- Faculty Appointment Department Recommendation Form must be signed by BOTH chairs
- Same for secondary/tertiary departments: signed form + CV/bibliography

## Promotion Documentation

### Prepared by the Department
1. Faculty Promotion Department Recommendation Form
2. Memo from department chair recommending promotion + rationale + proposed effective date
3. Current CV and bibliography
4. Current NYS Medical License Registration (if applicable)
5. American Board Certification Certificate(s) (if applicable)

### Joint Promotions
- Promotion Recommendation Form signed by BOTH chairs
- Each chair submits a separate memo

### Secondary/Tertiary Department Promotions
- Signed Promotion Recommendation Form (primary + secondary/tertiary chair)
- Memo from secondary/tertiary chair
- Current CV and bibliography
